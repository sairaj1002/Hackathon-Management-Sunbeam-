### HackHorizon – Dedicated Platform for Hackathon Management
### Overview
    HackHorizon is a web-based Hackathon Management System designed to streamline the complete hackathon lifecycle, from participant registration and team formation to project submission, judging, and result publication.
    The platform provides separate dashboards for Participants, Judges, and Administrators, ensuring an organized and transparent hackathon experience.

###    Problem Statement
    Managing hackathons manually can be time-consuming and prone to errors. Organizers often face challenges in handling registrations, team management, project submissions, judging, and result generation.
    HackHorizon provides a centralized platform that automates these processes, making hackathon management efficient, scalable, and transparent.
